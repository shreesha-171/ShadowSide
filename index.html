ShadowSide Route Map


      

        ShadowSide Route Map is a lightweight mapping system that visualizes how sunlight
        falls on different sides of your vehicle during a specific travel route and time.
        The system calculates LEFT / RIGHT / FRONT / BACK sun exposure using solar
        azimuth data and route geometry.
